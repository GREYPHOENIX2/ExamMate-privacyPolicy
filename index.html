Privacy Policy for ExamMate

ExamMate provides previous question papers for matric learners in South Africa. Our app helps students access past exam papers and study resources for educational purposes.

Information We Collect

We use Google’s AdMob advertising platform, which may collect your device’s advertising ID to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads. ExamMate itself does not collect any personally identifiable information from users.

How We Use Information

The advertising ID is used to display relevant ads and measure ad performance. We may also use aggregated analytics data from Google services to monitor app performance and improve functionality.

Data Sharing

The advertising ID is shared with Google AdMob for the purpose of serving ads and analyzing ad performance. No other user data is shared with third parties.

Children

This app is intended for matric learners and is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

User Rights

Users can reset or limit their device advertising ID in Android settings to opt out of personalized ads.
